Miles "Tails" Prower

About Tails

A main character in the Sonic the Hedgehog games and comics. Tails is a two-tailed fox who can fly and is an excellent inventor and engineer.

Tail's characteristics

  1. He can fly by spinning his tails.
  2. He flies a plane called the Tornado.
  3. His best friend is Sonic.
  4. He first appeared in Sonic the Hedgehog 2.
